🌟 The Festival Adventure 🌟

My name is Tomás. I am a student. I am 7 years old. I live in Neiva, Huila.

Today is the Festival del Bambuco! It is a big party in our city! 🎊 The music is beautiful!

I see my friend Valentina. She is a dancer. She is very good! Her dress is colorful. 💃

I also see Don Carlos. He is a musician. He plays the guitar near the Río Magdalena. 🎸🌊

My parents are farmers. They grow coffee in the mountains. ☕🌄 My mom says: "We are proud Huilenses!"

At the festival, I see a doctor helping people. She is kind. I also see a police officer. He is very tall! 👮

Tomás is happy. The festival is amazing! 🎉 "We are from Neiva and we are proud!"
